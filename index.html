Journey Toward Quantum Realities

My fascination with quantum mechanics began with its philosophical puzzles - the measurement problem, wavefunction collapse, and the nature of reality itself. This led me to hydrodynamic quantum analogs, where millimeter-scale droplets walking on vibrating fluids exhibit uncanny quantum-like behavior.

Currently collaborating with Rahil Valani (Oxford) on double-well potential dynamics, I'm working to bridge theoretical models with experimental observations of synchronization in walking droplets. Each small finding feels like decrypting nature's subtle hints about deeper quantum connections.
